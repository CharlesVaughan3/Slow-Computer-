------------------------------SLOW EMAIL------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

If your email has slow navigation and clicking between emails and the calendar takes forever do the following
steps to increase speed. 

1. Remove all Add-ins
	i. Open outlook > click file (top right) > options (second from bottom) > Add-ins(second from bottom) >
	   Click where it says go at the bottom of the open window (next to Manage: Com Add-ins) > Deselect all
	   add ins and click ok

2. Check mailbox size
	i. Open outlook > click file (top right) > Mailbox size is in middle of the window
	ii. If you are low on space and know a big chunk of emails that can be deleted that is the best way
	    to clear up space. If you need to write a rule to permanently delete email's older than a specific 
	    date this is possible too
		a. Make sure you are clicked on the mailbox that you would like to delete items in > 
		   Click rules (top of page on ribbon) > Manage rules and alerts > New Rule(top left of window)
		   > Set step 1 to receive in a specific date span and select the date you are looking for
		   > Set step 2 to stop processing more rules and permanently delete it > Click Next >
		   Select run this rule now on messages already in (Mailbox you have selected) > Click finish
